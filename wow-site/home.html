The Story

Prepare to be inspired,uplifted

and taken on a breathtaking journey.....

A montage of Theatre, Dance, Colour, Movement & Art..

A two hour show held anually in Wellington, NZ.

WOW® is a Brancott Estate Awards Show!

WOW® was first shown in 1987.

Nearly 150 garments created by world known designers.

WOW® began as a promotion for rural art gallery in Nelson

We need more quirky and colourful things in life - WOW Founder

Designers

We welcome the new designers and returning designers...

Click on photo to enter the particular section.

The Children's Section

Conceal and Reveal: Create a garment that transforms with an element of surprise.

American Express Open Section

This section has no thematic boundaries and gives the designer complete freedom.

CentrePort Illumination Illusion Section

Create a work of art in another dimension that glows under ultra violet light.

Tourism New Zealand Avant Garde Section

Where art and fashion collide! Create a wearable work of art that is revolutionary.

Gen-i Creative Excellence Section

Design a work of art that makes sound & will be choreographed to a music score.

Bizarre Bra® Section

Garments must be a bra only and and not include accessories. Must be a bra that fits the breast, not a bustier.

Air New Zealand South Pacific Section

Celebrate what it is like to live in New Zealand and the South Pacific in this section.
